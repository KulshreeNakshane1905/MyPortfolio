About Me

I am a dedicated and enthusiastic Full Stack Developer and Designer, currently pursuing my third year in Computer Engineering. With hands-on experience in developing and designing modern web applications, I bring a well-rounded blend of technical proficiency and creative problem-solving abilities.

My journey in the field of technology began with a strong curiosity for how digital systems work, which gradually evolved into a deep passion for building efficient, user-centric applications. Over the years, I have honed my skills in both front-end and back-end development, gaining practical knowledge in technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Machine Learning and various database systems.

In parallel, my interest in design has enabled me to understand the importance of user experience and visual appeal. I consistently create intuitive and aesthetically pleasing interfaces that enhance the overall functionality of the applications I develop. As a Computer Engineering student, I am constantly driven to expand my knowledge, explore emerging technologies, and contribute to impactful projects. I take pride in my ability to bridge the gap between design and development, and I am committed to delivering innovative digital solutions that meet both user and business needs.
